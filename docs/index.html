Tjaldur Software Governance Solutions is a company leading in the field of software governance and license compliance engineering, specifically GNU GPL and LGPL license compliance.

Our vision

We think that software governance does not have to be difficult, but can be done in a simple and easy way. Using the right processes, right tools and the right mindset it becomes a no-brainer.

We strongly believe in transparency and the ability to reproduce results and verify facts.

What we can do for you

We have many years of practical experience with software governance and GPL license compliance (since 2005) and have helped companies fix compliance issues and prevent costly lawsuits from either happening or escalating any further.
